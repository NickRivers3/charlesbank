Business:

Based in Wilmington, MA, Tecomet is a leading contract manufacturer of orthopedic implants and instruments, as well as critical components for the cardiovascular, medical imaging and aerospace markets. Its core capabilities include forging, precision machining and photochemical etching.

Investment:

In 2006, Charlesbank partnered with two prior portfolio company executives, Bill Dow and John Connolly, to complete an extensive search for medical device contract-manufacturing investment opportunities. After reviewing more than two dozen companies, we acquired Tecomet from Cardinal Health in 2008, in partnership with existing management. Bill Dow became the new CEO and John Connolly joined as Executive Vice President and CFO.

Our Role:

Charlesbank worked closely with management to carve out the company from its prior corporate owner, refine its long-term growth plan, expand the sales force, identify manufacturing efficiencies and evaluate multiple acquisition opportunities.

Outcome:

In December 2013, Charlesbank completed the sale of Tecomet to Genstar Capital. During our ownership, Tecomet completed and integrated two strategic acquisitions that expanded its capabilities and customer base. These acquisitions, coupled with organic growth, allowed the company to nearly triple its EBITDA. The sales force also grew from 7 to 21, and Tecomet successfully built on its reputation for innovation and customer responsiveness.
