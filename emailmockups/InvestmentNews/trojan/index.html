Business:

Trojan Battery is a global leader in energy storage solutions and one of the world’s largest manufacturers of deep-cycle lead-acid batteries. Its products are designed to meet the complex energy storage requirements of markets such as fleet golf, aerial work platform, floor cleaning, renewable energy, backup power and transportation. The Trojan brand is recognized throughout the world as a symbol of quality, reliability and value. Manufactured in state-of-the-art facilities, the company’s products are sold in over 120 countries.

Investment:

In late June 2013, Charlesbank made an investment in Trojan Battery, marking the first outside equity the company has ever taken. The management team made a significant co-investment, and the Godber family, which founded the company in 1925, maintains a significant ownership position.

About Us:

Based in Boston and New York, Charlesbank Capital Partners is a middle-market private equity investment firm managing more than $2 billion of capital.
