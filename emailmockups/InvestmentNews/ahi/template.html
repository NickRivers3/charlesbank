Business:

Founded in 1954 and based in Westlake, TX, Animal Health International is one of the largest distributors of animal health products in North America. The company purchases a wide array of pharmaceuticals, biologicals and other animal health-care products from more than 1,500 manufacturers. AHI’s diverse customer base includes livestock producers and owners, veterinarians and retail dealers across the United States and Canada.

Investment:

In 2005, we approached the owner and, together with management, negotiated the purchase of the company, with Charlesbank acquiring a controlling interest. To fund the transaction, we also arranged debt financing from a consortium of lenders.

Our Role:

During our ownership, we partnered with AHI management to evolve the business both strategically and financially, working closely to develop a growth strategy, accomplish several important acquisitions and guide the company through a successful IPO.

Outcome:

In the period of our involvement, AHI significantly increased profitability through various organic growth initiatives (including expanding its companion animal sales and introducing new private-label products) and through the acquisition of eight companies totatling approximately $100 million of revenue. With strong growth and profitability, AHI was able to complete a dividend recapitalization and an IPO in 2006 and 2007, respectively, returning a multiple of our invested capital.

In June 2011, we monetized the remainder of our ownership stake in connection with a sale of AHI to Lextron, a strategic acquirer. This sale, combined with proceeds previously returned, generated a return in excess of 3x Charlesbank’s investment.
