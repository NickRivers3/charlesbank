Business: OnCore Manufacturing is a leading provider of high-complexity electronics manufacturing services. Serving blue-chip OEM customers in the defense, healthcare and industrial markets across North America, OnCore focuses on design and manufacture of electronic assemblies as well as system integration. Its products are used in applications such as encrypted mobile communications, radar systems, medical equipment and high-speed broadband infrastructure.

Investment:

 In December 2010, Charlesbank purchased OnCore with a significant coinvestment from the existing management team. To fund the acquisition, Charlesbank also arranged the debt financing, which included an asset-backed revolver and term loan.
Headquarters: Springfield, MA
CEO: Dan Perez
Employees: 1,025
Facilities: Four plants in the US, one in Mexico

About Us:

Based in Boston and New York, Charlesbank Capital Partners is a middle-market private equity investment firm managing more than $2 billion of capital. Charlesbank focuses on management-led buyouts and growth capital financings, typically investing $50 million to $150 million per transaction in companies with enterprise values of $100 million to $750 million. The firm seeks to partner with strong management teams to build companies with sustainable competitive advantages and excellent prospects for growth.
