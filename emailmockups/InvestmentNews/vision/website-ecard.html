Business:

Vision Source is the leading independent optometric franchising organization in North America, with a network of over 2,300 franchises and 4,500 optometrists across all 50 US states and Canada. Vision Source offers its members attractive purchasing power, professional and staff development, and practice management assistance, while allowing optometrists to maintain independence. The company is led by an experienced management team that has grown revenue and earnings every year since beginning operations.
Charlesbank Role:

Vision Source was originally acquired by Charlesbank and H.I.G. Capital as part of their purchase of parent company TLC Vision, North America’s premier eye-care services company. With the backing of Charlesbank and H.I.G., TLC emerged from Chapter 11 in May 2010 as a newly reorganized, private company with its balance sheet deleveraged and capital availability for future expansion. Charlesbank helped Vision Source navigate through TLC’s bankruptcy and assisted in the sale process. From the outset, Charlesbank’s goal was to streamline TLC’s operations and focus on its core businesses. Consistent with this strategy, the Vision Source sale further strengthens TLC’s financial condition, returns significant proceeds to Charlesbank and positions TLC to grow rapidly.
